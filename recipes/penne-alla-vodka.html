Penne alla vodka

Perfectly hearty and tender penne pasta is tossed with a creamy and vibrant, San Marzano tomato based and vodka infused sauce that is seasoned with fresh herbs and parmesan.

Ingredients

  • 1 (28 oz) can San Marzano tomatoes (crushed finely with hands)
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Yellow onion - finely minced
  • Garlic - freshly minced
  • Vodka
  • Salt and pepper
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Heavy cream
  • Fresh basil and fresh oregano
  • Shredded parmesan cheese
  • Penne pasta

Cooking instructions

  1. Boil water.
  2. Heat oil and saute aromatics.
  3. Add tomatoes and vodka. Simmer until strong alcohol scent has cooked off.
  4. Cook pasta.
  5. Mix cream, herbs and parmesan into sauce.
  6. Toss vodka sauce and penne.
